# Special Cases

## Invoices with Embedded Credits (e.g. UBC Rec)

Some vendors (such as UBC Rec) issue invoices that include both a gross charge and registration or sales credits, so only a **net amount** is debited from the GSS account.

**In Zoho Expense:** Upload the invoice and enter only the net amount actually charged. In the description, note the gross cost and the credit applied (e.g. *"6 classes × $140 = $840 gross; $760 registration credits applied; $80 net"*).

**After uploading:** Email [accounting@gss.ubc.ca](mailto:accounting@gss.ubc.ca) so they can create manual journal entries to record the full income and expense amounts against the correct budget lines.

{: .important }
> Actuals will appear lower than budget for both revenue and expenses — this is expected and corrected through journal entries.
