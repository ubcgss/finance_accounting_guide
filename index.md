## About This Guide

This guide is maintained by the Financial Officer and House-Finance Committee. It draws from the GSS Bylaws, House-Finance Policy, and the Accounting and Procedures manual to provide clear, actionable steps for staff.

The GSS fiscal year runs from **June 1 to May 31**, with quarters:

| Quarter | Months |
|---------|--------|
| Q1 | June — August |
| Q2 | September — November |
| Q3 | December — February |
| Q4 | March — May |
